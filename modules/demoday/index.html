Demo Day information

Demo Day will be on Wednesday, May 9th from 5-7 PM at GitHub HQ, 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107. Please clear your calendars in advance; this is the culmination of all Mission Bit courses throughout San Francisco. It's a celebration of all the hard work you've done throughout the semester. You'll show off your projects to peers and industry professionals, make connections, dinner will be served, and a few student projects will be selected for cash prizes. Invite your friends and family! I will post more guidelines about presentations and detailed information here when it becomes available.
